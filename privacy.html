Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Productivity Tracker extension ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Active Tab Monitoring: We collect data on the currently active tab in your browser to track your online activities and provide real-time information and alerts.
  • Storage: We store your preferences, settings, and historical usage data to ensure you can access your tracked data and configure the extension according to your preferences.
  • Web History: We collect a list of web pages you have visited, along with associated data such as page titles and times of visits.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Monitor and track your online activities to enhance your productivity.
  • Provide real-time information and alerts based on your browsing behavior.
  • Store and manage your preferences, settings, and historical usage data.

Host Permissions

Host permissions specify which websites or domains the extension is allowed to interact with. The extension operates only on the specific websites defined by you, respecting your preferences for tracking and restrictions.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
